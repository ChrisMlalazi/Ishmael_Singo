Ishmael Singo born 16-04-1986 Studied at Mzilikazi Art and Craft Centre 2003-2004. Became resident artist at the National Gallery in Bulawayo 2005-2009 Participated in a number of group exhibitions just to name a few "Young artist Exhibition " Gallery Delta Harare 2007, "Thatha Nwele" NGB 2005... Had my first solo exhibition 2013 "Interface" National Gallery in Bulawayo. Awards; Young artist of promise 2006 "VAAB Annual Exhibition. Best artist under 30 " Annual Wild Geese Art Festival" Harare 2012... i do fine art, ....im inspired by our day to day way of living, nature....i love painting figures and buildings and also wild life my favourite visual artist is Kaufman Ndlovu
